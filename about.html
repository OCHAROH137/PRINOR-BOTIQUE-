About PRINOR BOUTIQUE

Discover our story, vision, and commitment to bringing you the finest fashion and footwear

Our Story

Founded in 2020, PRINOR BOUTIQUE began as a dream to create a premium fashion destination that combines style, quality, and affordability. What started as a small family business has grown into a trusted name in fashion retail across Kenya.

We believe that everyone deserves to look and feel their best, which is why we carefully curate our collection to include only the finest pieces from renowned designers and emerging talents alike.

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond our products to encompass exceptional customer service, sustainable practices, and giving back to our community.

Our Vision

To become the leading boutique destination in Kenya where fashion meets functionality, and where every customer finds their perfect style expression.

Quality First

We source only the highest quality materials and work with trusted manufacturers to ensure durability and style.

Customer Focused

Your satisfaction is our priority, and we go above and beyond to exceed expectations with personalized service.

Sustainable Fashion

We're committed to environmentally responsible practices and ethical sourcing in all our operations.
