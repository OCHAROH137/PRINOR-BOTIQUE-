Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 14-day return policy for all unworn items in original condition with tags attached. Returns are free for defective items, and a small fee of KES 200 applies for other returns.

How long does delivery take in Nairobi?

Standard delivery within Nairobi takes 1-2 business days, while express delivery takes same day or next day. Free delivery is available on orders over KES 5,000.

Do you deliver outside Nairobi?

Yes, we deliver to all major towns in Kenya. Delivery takes 2-5 business days depending on location. Delivery charges vary by destination.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive an SMS with tracking details. You can also call our customer service line for updates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, Airtel Money, bank transfers, and cash on delivery. Credit/debit cards are also accepted for online payments.

Do you have a physical store?

Yes, our flagship store is located on Kimathi Street in Nairobi CBD. You can visit us to try on items and get personalized styling advice.
